Welcome to RecyShred

Our smart Recycling Bin rewards users with coins based on the weight of recyclable materials. After shredding, coins are earned and can be redeemed for various rewards.

How It Works

1. Insert Material

Insert recyclable paper, cans, or bottles.

2. Shred & Measure

The system shreds and measures the material.

3. Redeem Rewards

Check your dashboard and redeem rewards when you have enough coins.
